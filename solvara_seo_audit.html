01
Technical SEO

Lighthouse was run against both the homepage and the flagship Shilajit product page. The site is technically healthy from a speed perspective (desktop) but several structural and best-practice issues need addressing before Google can effectively crawl and trust the site.

No Organic Rankings Detected — Brand-New Domain with Zero Authority
CRITICAL

DataForSEO Labs returned zero ranked keywords for solvara.co.uk in the UK. This confirms the site has not yet earned organic visibility for any query. The domain is brand-new and Google has yet to index/rank product pages.

Action Submit the sitemap immediately via Google Search Console. Ensure all product pages are indexable. Begin link acquisition (see Off-Page section). This is the single biggest growth lever.
Lighthouse Best Practices Score: 69/100 — Third-Party Cookies & Security Headers
CRITICAL

The Best Practices score of 69 (homepage) and 73 (product page) indicates issues with third-party scripts — most likely Shopify's shop.app widget and payment processor scripts setting insecure cookies or lacking HTTPS-only headers. Google uses Best Practices signals as tie-breakers.

Action Audit third-party scripts in Shopify settings. Remove or defer non-essential apps. Add Content-Security-Policy and X-Frame-Options headers via a Cloudflare proxy or Shopify custom liquid. Review shop.app inclusion — it's adding 16MB+ to homepage payload.
Homepage Total Byte Weight: 16.9 MB — Severely Over-bloated
CRITICAL

Lighthouse reports a total page weight of 16,896,981 bytes (~16.9 MB) on the homepage. This is extremely high and strongly indicates an unoptimised hero video. The product page is a much healthier 2.1 MB. On mobile connections this homepage would be unusable.

Action Convert the hero video to a compressed WebM/MP4 under 3 MB. Use loading="lazy" for below-fold images. Consider replacing the video with a high-quality static image on mobile via Shopify's responsive section settings. Target total page weight under 3 MB.
Main-thread Work: 2,658ms on Product Pages — JS Execution Bottleneck
MEDIUM

While LCP (1.3s) and TTI (1.3s) are acceptable on desktop, the product page main-thread work of 2,658ms will cause real performance problems on mobile devices. The unused-javascript score (80ms flagged) suggests bloated Shopify theme scripts.

Action Audit installed Shopify apps — each app adds JS. Remove unused apps. Defer non-critical scripts using defer and async attributes. Consider moving to a performance-optimised theme like Dawn or a headless setup long-term.
No Collections Page / Category Architecture — Missing SEO Landing Pages
MEDIUM

The site navigates directly from the homepage to individual product pages via a dropdown "Shop" menu. There are no collections pages (e.g., /collections/ayurvedic-supplements, /collections/adaptogens) to target category-level keywords. This is a significant structural gap for a supplement brand.

Action Create Shopify Collections for: "Ayurvedic Supplements UK", "Adaptogen Powders", "Shilajit Products", "Women's Health Supplements". These pages can rank for high-volume commercial terms that individual product pages cannot.
No Blog / Content Hub — Zero Informational Traffic Pathway
MEDIUM

There is no blog or articles section. In the supplements/wellness space, informational content (e.g., "shilajit benefits", "ashwagandha for stress", 27K–165K monthly searches) is a primary organic traffic driver and builds topical authority that lifts product page rankings.

Action Enable Shopify Blog. Create a "Wellness Journal" or "The Solvara Edit" content hub. Prioritise 6–8 pillar articles targeting high-volume informational keywords (see Keyword Mapping section).
No Schema Markup Detected — Missing Product, Review & FAQ Rich Results
MEDIUM

No structured data (JSON-LD) was found on product pages. Shopify's default theme sometimes outputs basic Product schema, but it's not confirmed here. Missing: Product schema with AggregateRating, FAQPage schema, and BreadcrumbList. Rich snippets (star ratings in SERPs) significantly improve CTR.

Action Install a Shopify Schema app (e.g., SEO King, Yotpo) or manually add JSON-LD blocks in product page liquid templates. Prioritise Product + Review schema first, then FAQ schema using the product Q&A content already on pages.
Core Web Vitals: Excellent on Desktop (LCP 1.0s homepage, 1.3s product page)
GOOD

LCP (Largest Contentful Paint): 1.01s homepage / 1.34s product page. CLS (Cumulative Layout Shift): 0 / 0.003. FID proxy (Max Potential FID): 24ms / 23ms. All well within Google's "Good" thresholds on desktop. Server response time is fast at 22ms (homepage).

HTTPS, Shopify CDN, and Clean URL Structure in Place
GOOD

The site uses HTTPS throughout, Shopify's global CDN for assets, and has clean URL slugs (/products/pure-shilajit-resin, /products/ashwagandha-root-powder). No redirect chains detected on crawled pages.

Homepage Has No H1 Tag
CRITICAL

The homepage HTML contains no <h1> element. The brand tagline "At Solvara, we don't just source botanicals" is rendered as an <h2>. An H1 is a critical on-page signal for Google to understand the page's primary topic.

Recommended H1 "Ayurvedic Supplements & Botanicals | UK" — or a variant that leads with the brand's hero product category and includes the UK market identifier.
Homepage Title Tag Contains Zero Search Keywords
CRITICAL

The current title "Solvara | Refined by Nature, Elevated by Purity" is a brand tagline, not an SEO title. It contains no searchable terms. Google rewrites titles it deems uninformative, often replacing them with H1 or body text — which in this case is also not keyword-rich.

Recommended Title "Solvara | Pure Ayurvedic Supplements & Shilajit Resin — UK" (55 chars). This leads with brand, contains primary category keyword, flagship product, and geo-modifier.
All Product Title Tags Are Missing UK Geo-Modifier and Benefit-Driven Language
MEDIUM

For a UK-focused brand competing against Amazon, Holland & Barrett, and rawliving.co.uk, the geo-modifier "UK" in title tags is a meaningful differentiator in SERPs. Titles like "Pure Shilajit Resin – Solvara" are too short (28 chars vs ideal 55–60) and lack conversion-intent language.

Recommended Titles
Shilajit: "Pure Himalayan Shilajit Resin UK | 80% Fulvic Acid — Solvara" (62 chars)
Ashwagandha: "Organic Ashwagandha Root Powder UK | Stress & Energy — Solvara" (63 chars)
Moringa: "Moringa Leaf Powder UK | Pure Organic Botanical — Solvara" (57 chars)
No Meta Descriptions Set on Product Pages
MEDIUM

Meta descriptions are not a ranking factor but directly impact CTR. Google will auto-generate them from page copy if not set — often pulling sub-optimal snippets. In a competitive supplement SERP, a compelling meta description can be the difference between a click and a scroll-past.

Example for Shilajit page "Sourced at 18,000ft in the Himalayas. Our premium Gold Grade Shilajit Resin contains 80% Fulvic Acid & 85+ trace minerals. Lab-tested. Free UK delivery over £59.99." (160 chars)
"Need Help?" Accordion Section Contains Template Placeholder Text
MEDIUM

Both the Shilajit and Ashwagandha product pages contain an accordion section labelled "Need Help?" with entries reading "Use this text to answer questions in as much detail as possible for your customers." This is unpublished placeholder copy and presents an unprofessional experience. More importantly, this section is an ideal location for keyword-rich FAQ content that can earn FAQ rich results in Google.

Action Replace all four accordion items with real, keyword-rich FAQs per product. Example for Shilajit: "How do I use Shilajit Resin?", "What is the difference between Shilajit resin and powder?", "Is Shilajit legal in the UK?", "How long until I see results?". These also answer high-intent Google searches.
URL Slugs Are Clean, Descriptive, and Keyword-Appropriate
GOOD

All product URL slugs are clean and contain the primary product keyword: /products/pure-shilajit-resin, /products/ashwagandha-root-powder, etc. No query strings, no ID numbers — exactly right for SEO.

Product Copy Is Detailed, Well-Structured, and Contains Naturally Relevant Keywords
GOOD

The long-form product descriptions are genuinely excellent. The Shilajit page covers: sourcing, fulvic acid content, gold trace minerals, usage instructions, storage, allergens, and key benefits in well-formatted sections. This depth is a strong on-page SEO signal once the domain gains authority.

Conversion Rate Optimisation

CRO analysis across four page types: Homepage, Collection/Category (currently absent), Product Page, and Blog template (currently absent). Visual mockups show recommended structural improvements.

6.1 — Homepage CRO
Hero Section: Video-Only with No Immediate Value Proposition or CTA Above the Fold
HIGH IMPACT

The homepage hero is a full-screen video with no overlaid text CTA, no headline explaining what Solvara sells, and no "Shop Now" button. Visitors who land without brand awareness have no immediate direction. The brand tagline appears below the fold.

Fix Overlay the hero video with: (1) a short, punchy H1 e.g. "Ancient Botanicals. Modern Purity."; (2) a 1-line subheadline naming the product category; (3) dual CTAs: "Shop Shilajit" (primary) + "Explore All Botanicals" (secondary); (4) 3 trust badges below the fold line.
No Trust Signals in First Viewport — Lab Certifications Are Buried
MEDIUM IMPACT

Solvara's most powerful differentiator — third-party UK lab-tested certificates — is accessible only via the "Authenticity" menu. Trust signals like "Lab Certified", "UK Lab Tested", "Third-Party Verified" should appear within the first scroll on the homepage and on every product page.

Fix Add a thin trust bar immediately below the hero (or within it): "✓ UK Lab Certified ✓ Third-Party Tested ✓ Free UK Delivery over £59.99 ✓ Himalayan Sourced". This bar is visible on the recommended wireframe below.
No Reviews / Social Proof on Homepage
MEDIUM IMPACT

There is a /pages/our-customers page but no testimonials, star ratings, or review counts visible on the homepage or product pages. In the supplement category, social proof is one of the top conversion drivers.

Fix Add a reviews carousel section to the homepage using Shopify's native reviews app or Judge.me. Display star rating averages on product cards. Minimum target: 10 verified reviews per product before active SEO push.

6.2 — Product Page CRO
No Reviews / Star Ratings on Product Pages — #1 Conversion Killer
HIGH IMPACT

There are zero reviews displayed on any product page. No star ratings, no customer quotes, no review count. For a supplement brand, this is the single biggest conversion barrier — customers need social proof before purchasing health products.

Fix Install Judge.me (free Shopify app), email existing customers for reviews, and display star rating in the product header within 1 week. Target 20+ reviews per top-selling product.
No Urgency, Scarcity, or Risk-Reversal Elements
HIGH IMPACT

The Shilajit product page ATC button is functional but surrounded by zero conversion-boosting elements: no "In Stock", no satisfaction guarantee badge, no free delivery callout near the button, no "X units sold" social proof.

Fix Add immediately below the ATC button: (1) a green lock-up "🔒 30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee"; (2) "✓ In Stock — Dispatched within 24hrs"; (3) "🚚 Free UK delivery on orders over £59.99".
No Subscription / Subscribe & Save Option Visible
MEDIUM IMPACT

The page code references a "deferred, subscription, or recurring purchase" but there is no visible subscription pricing option in the product page UI. Shilajit is a daily supplement — subscription revenue is critical for LTV and should be prominently offered (e.g., "Subscribe & Save 15%").

Fix Add a one-time vs subscribe toggle above the Add to Cart button using Shopify's native subscription features or Recharge. Show the price saving clearly. This is a major AOV and LTV lever.
